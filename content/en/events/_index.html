They will host these two days of meetings and exchanges with us:
The Laboratory of Sustainable Practices
The Sustainable Practices Laboratory is a programme supported by the National manufactures with the support of the
Bettencourt-Schueller Foundation which aims in particular to identify, support, and
disseminate projects on new sustainable materials and eco-responsible colors.

JAÙH Laboratory
From the principles of creative residences for
designers and artisans of  Maison JAÙH , a cabinet publishing house that
explores the generative algorithm, the laboratory is a division dedicated to
projects, applied research, and sharing in
becoming knowledge.

Leca Sevestre Avocats
Jean-Baptiste Leca and Carlyne Sevestre founded the law firm Leca Sevestre Avocats to support creators and companies in their innovative and creative projects at the intersection of design, technology, and industry.  

Ap'Innovation
Ap'Innovation  is an innovation consulting firm. It provides support in the study and implementation of the main innovation financing mechanisms, according to needs.

Campus MaNa
Campus MaNa

 offers immersive, residential professional training courses. It is a place of learning and creation, a Qualiopi-certified training centre born from the dream of designer Thomas Dariel and Sophie Dariel and based on experiential learning. It brings together professionals from the fields of art, design and innovation, alongside master craftsmen with rare skills, and welcomes visitors from all over the world. Campus MaNa was established in Burgundy, two hours from Paris. It is now continuing its international development with the opening of Campus MaNa Venezia in 2025, on the island of San Servolo in Venice, Italy. Find out about the courses available on their website: https://campusmana.com  

Campus Versailles
Inaugurated in 2021 in the heart of the Grande Écurie at the Palace of Versailles, the campus is both a network and a place to discover, practise and train in heritage and craft trades. A non-profit association, Campus Versailles brings together and animates a community of public and private actors: education professionals, heritage companies, artisans, students and institutions, who pool their resources to promote the professions, accelerate the upskilling of skills and create an exceptional training system.
