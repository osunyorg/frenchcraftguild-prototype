Created in 2012 at the initiative of the City of Paris, the  Fonds pour les Ateliers de Paris  aims to support the development of companies in the design, fashion and craft sectors by strengthening the actions deployed by the City of Paris. To this end, the Fund seeks and collects private donations from corporate sponsors or partner foundations, as well as donations from individuals.

Our vision

Bringing ever higher the excellence of know-how and talented creators is the vision defended by the Fonds pour les Ateliers de Paris.

Three strong values

  • Promoting innovation and a sustainable business environment
  • Facilitating the transfer of know-how
  • Cultivate professional networks in France and internationally

Our ambition

Along with the Bureau du Design, de la Mode et des Métiers d'Art and with the support of patrons, the Fonds pour les Ateliers de Paris works to finance the development and enhancement of the creative professions.

These unique know-how contribute to our history and culture throughout the world.

"Since 2012, our ambition has been to enable design, fashion and craft professionals to train, create and grow in their local environment. We aspire to encourage sustainable creations that enhance everyday life, to support artisans and businesses in hiring and transmitting their know-how, and to strengthen Paris' position as the world capital of innovation and creative exchanges."

Lyne Cohen-Solal, President of the Fonds pour les Ateliers de Paris
