Atelier Steven Leprizé is a creative studio and cabinetmaking workshop combining exceptional craftsmanship and innovation. Founded and directed by Steven Leprizé, the studio has been operating since a cutting-edge workshop near Paris, France. From design to on-site installation, the studio offers complete support, with a tailor-made and multidisciplinary approach. The 600 m2 workshop is equipped with high-tech wood machining machines, CNC milling machines, a 3D printer and tools adapted to the work of multiple materials, allowing the team to realize the most daring ideas. In close collaboration with interior architects, designers and international brands, Steven Leprizé brings to life complex custom projects.
