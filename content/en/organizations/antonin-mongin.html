Within his workshop, Antonin Mongin designs and produces artisanal textile materials with rare and singular fibres such as hair (synthetic or natural), horsehair, raffia, sisal and ramie - silkscreen, felt, weaving, mesh and 3D resin printing - for private individuals or luxury homes (clothes, accessories and jewellery). He is also an R&D consultant for these houses and author for publications related to the field of crafts.
