Juliette Vivien is a French ceramicist specializing in openwork porcelain, creating hand-crafted craft pieces in her workshop in the Paris region. Initiated very young in ceramics, she trained at the École Duperré and founded her business in 2018. His subtle and contemporary work was rewarded with the Ateliers d’Art de France's Prize of Young Creation in 2020.
