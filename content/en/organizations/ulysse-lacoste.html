body: >-

Les Soufrières - Checkers game

The game board was created using the deep-drawing technique, a stretching process that takes advantage of the malleability of metal. Unlike traditional metalworking techniques, the material is left undamaged. With a repetitive, geometric pattern inspired by the mashrabiya, the chosen motif is that of a checkerboard. Chrome-plated steel balls bring this uneven terrain to life.

Copper, steel, and magnets - 35x35cm
