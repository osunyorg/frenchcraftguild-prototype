body: >-


Index Office is a design, creation and design studio that develops a sensitive approach to contemporary technologies. The studio's research areas focus on industrial design on the scale of the object of everyday life, furniture and scenographic space. The studio is driven by a constant search for functionality, durability, ownership and timelessness. Founded in 2019 by Nelson Fossey, Index Office has initiated long-term collaborations with various manufacturers, craftsmen, architects, publishers and institutions to design innovative and tailor-made solutions.
