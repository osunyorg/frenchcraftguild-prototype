body: >-

Index Office is a strategic and creative design studio that develops a sensitive approach to contemporary technologies. The studio's field of research focus on industrial design at the scale of everyday life products, furnitures, and scenography. The studio is driven by a constant search for functionality, sustainability, appropriation, and timelessness.Founded in 2019 by Nelson Fossey, Index Office has initiated long-term collaborations with various manufacturers, artisans, architects, publishers, and institutions to design innovative and tailor-made solutions.
