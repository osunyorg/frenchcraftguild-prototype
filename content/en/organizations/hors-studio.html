Founded by Elodie Michaud and Rebecca Fezard in 2016, hors-studio specializes in textiles, materials and surfaces. They join forces to create their design studio, born of the same vision and ambitions. By comparing tradition with new technologies, their practice questions the fields of textile and decorative language. The studio focuses on the choice and processing of materials, their ennobling, and addresses projects in a transversal way at the border between design, fashion, art-de-vivre, graphics and space.
