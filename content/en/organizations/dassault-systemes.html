Ta.tamu
To design a chair that could be manufactured using as little material as possible, it was necessary to innovate in the design process itself and use cutting-edge virtual prototyping solutions: topological optimization, generative design, simulation, life cycle assessment, etc. Ta.tamu is printed folded, a world first.
