Hortus Botanica
The Hortus Botanica series was born of a simple desire: a figurative form, fruit of the memory of the orchard and pleasure of harvest. A luminous still nature that combines two crafts: glass blowing and chiseling. Limited series.
— Botanica Péponida lamp, 2025, limited series, chiseled brass and blown glass, about 15 cm x 18 cm. Made in collaboration with glassmaker Florian Harling. — Botanica Mirabella lamp, 2025, limited series, chiseled brass and blown glass, about 20cmx15cm. Made in collaboration with glassmaker Florian Harling.

A graduate of the Boulle School's scissors workshop in Paris, Kim Jude has refined her know-how with renowned artisans in France and abroad. In 2019, she created her own workshop, where she brought together with elegance the worlds of jewellery, lighting and decoration. It combines finely chiseled brass with blown glass, giving birth to both precious and poetic pieces.
