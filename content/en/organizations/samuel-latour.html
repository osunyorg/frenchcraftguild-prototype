Samuel Latour is a sculptor based in Rabastens, near Toulouse, where he lives and works. A graduate of École Boulle in Paris, he specialized in art shooting, especially on wood and bronze. He then enriched his practice by collaborating with designers, artisans and artists in workshops and art foundries.
