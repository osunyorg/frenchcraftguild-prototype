Through various initiatives, the Bureau du Design, de la Mode et des Métiers d'art supports emerging and established companies and creators in developing their businesses.

In collaboration with professional and institutional partners, the Office brings together and promotes the Parisian ecosystem of local creation and manufacturing, working to raise its profile in France and internationally.
