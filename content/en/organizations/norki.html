Home of artisanal know-how applied to precious materials, Norki shapes hides and furs to transform them into objects dedicated to the art of living. Labeled a Living Heritage Company (EPV), it relies on its three poles of excellence: the creation of decorative skin and fur objects, upholstery and custom-made interior architecture.
