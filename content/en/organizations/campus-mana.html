Campus MaNa provides immersive professional training programs on-site, integrating both transmission and creation. Established by designers Thomas Dariel and Sophie Dariel, this Qualiopi-certified center emphasizes experiential learning and unites professionals in the fields of arts and crafts, design, and innovation with master craftsmen who possess exceptional expertise.Born in Burgundy, two hours from Paris, Campus MaNa has expanded with the opening in 2025 of Campus MaNa Venezia, located on the island of San Servolo in Venice, Italy.
