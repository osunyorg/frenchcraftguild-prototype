Ambassador of the French art of living, Rinck is a company of interior architecture and design whose singularity is to integrate production workshops. The brand brings together the worlds of creation and realization. Thanks to the combination of these different professions, Rinck's creativity is based on the mastery of its teams and the expertise drawn from its traditions. Almost two-hundred-year-old know-how for prestigious projects, either alone or in partnership.

body: >-

Chair throne Opus Memoria - first piece
From the Opus Memoria collection, inspired by the atmosphere of a demiurge artist's workshop, this armchair stands out for its impressive appearance. It symbolizes and is part of a place where creativity is spreading, inviting exploration and imagination. A result of an unprecedented research work, specific attention is paid to the details and quality of the materials, to create both functional and artistic furniture. This foundation tells a story, that of a creator exploring forms, textures and materials to compose a rich and inspiring universe. First copy of a series of 8, it is designed to offer a unique experience, its design allows different sitting positions. It combines a solid sanded wood structure with a high-end leather coating whose drrape resembles a royal trail.

Miniature prototype Armchair throne
A true proof of concept, this miniature prototype was used as a preparatory work in the research process, validating the possibility of sheathing a hull created by a solidified drape. The idea of keeping a natural drape, like a nonchalently thrown skin, in the first copy of our series of 8, is to go through several stages of development: proportions, techniques, finishes. The apparent simplicity of this foundation, the research work necessary to achieve this, all this also makes our specificity: a capacity to unite our know-how, and associated know-how, to propose multi-material pieces of exception.
