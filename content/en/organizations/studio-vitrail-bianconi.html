Stained glass - Monochrome composition

Inspired by the geometric forms of the 1930s, this piece presents a modern interpretation of stained glass, utilizing a palette intentionally restricted to black, white, and grey. Textured, lacquered, opalescent, or silvered glass surfaces generate a dynamic interplay of transparencies, reflections, and depths, transforming light into a material of its own.
