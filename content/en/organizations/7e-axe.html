Jean-Marie Ollivier, passionate about machines, became an engineer in 2001. He worked for 20 years in the robotics industry. His love of art and creativity led him to create, alongside his engineering career, Le Bistromatik, a former industrial robot converted into a bartender, which he unveiled to the public in 2016. The success of this machine led him to found 7e Axe in 2023, a company specializing in the creation of industrial entertainment machines.
