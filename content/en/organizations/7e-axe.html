Founded in 2023 by Jean-Marie Ollivier, an engineer from Rennes, 7e Axe explores the links between the arts and robotics.
An engineer with a passion for machines, Jean-Marie Ollivier specialises in industrial robotics, designing and building machines whose movements are inspired by human gestures.
7e Axe is particularly well known for having developed a painting robot for Maison Hennessy (LVMH Group).
Each of his creations deciphers hand movements and translates them into robotic movements.
