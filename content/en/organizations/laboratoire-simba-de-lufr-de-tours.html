The research themes developed within the Simba team focus on the development of bioactive molecules for cancerology and infectious disease. A first fundamental axis is the development of new pathways to these molecules by synthesis or isolation from natural resources. The second axis applied is the biological evaluation of molecules synthesized or isolated in the laboratory (anti-cancer, anthelminthic, antifungal, antibacterial).
