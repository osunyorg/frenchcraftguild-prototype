An event of general interest

PROTOTYPE is a concept protected, designed and proposed by the French Craft Guild, a nonprofit organization enhancing French creative heritage. The event is organised and led by a collective of actors with a common mission: the exploitation of French know-how, as well as innovation of excellence. Partners as exhibitors, supporters as visitors: all contribute to a collective dynamic, open and eager for new approaches that create value.

An invested and experienced team

Project team gathered around PROTOTYPE  is composed of experienced event and communication professionals B to B to C, in the field of innovation, and more specifically design and know-how.
