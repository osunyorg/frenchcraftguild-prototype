Between experimentation and innovation, concept and creation, the prototype concretizes an idea and enables us to measure its potential at functional and aesthetic levels. It opens up new markets. It is also a dialogue interface between disciplines, for new collaborations and projects. PROTOTYPE is part of this dynamic to make it, from now on, an event.

Background
From the production chain to the value chain
Purpose, ethics, environmental and social impact... the need for meaning emerges everywhere. Especially in our consumer patterns. The current challenges of relocation and sustainability require transformation of our production methods and transitions.

L’innovation pour réinventer, en mieux
Changer les paradigmes et réinvestir des champs de recherche porteurs pour le plus grand nombre implique de repenser, reprendre, réinventer. Donc d'innover au sens propre et historique du terme. Il n'est pas question que de technologies : des processus métiers au Nation Branding, chaque étape est concernée, à chaque échelle.  

AI, impact multiplier
Digital, and more recently artificial intelligence, is both a creative means and an end. Although immaterial, digital today offers the ability to project itself into a real sensory experience to offer emotion, use and service. Most importantly, AI increases the impact of projects. A new creative partner, he extends the imagination, able to dialogue with human intuition and reveal unsuspecting potentials.

Observation
Towards further progress
For centuries, science, art, design, engineering, industry and research have been intertwined to create bold ideas and significant inventions. This excellence rests on a fertile soil, nourished by a humanist tradition, a high-level education, and constant support for creation and research. France is not only a country of ideas: it is a factory of the future, where each discipline feeds the other to develop innovations that have a lasting influence on the world.

Know-how as catalysts for innovation
France has a wealth in the basement: a deposit of exceptional know-how. Their lasting character, rooted in history, geography, resource, territory, memory... gives them enormous creative potential, crossed with new uses and markets.

A new generation of creators is emerging
Whether they are alone, in collective or in company, it is a French-style "Craft" that recreates the ingredients necessary for the creation of new models: more eco-conscious, more narrative, more committed...they work in a transdisciplinary way towards a common profit.

Criteria
Apply
Do you want to exhibit? Do you have a successful prototype, the result of an innovative approach in relation to know-how? Contact us!
