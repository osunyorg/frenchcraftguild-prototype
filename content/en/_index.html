February 5 & 6, 2026 - Hôtel de l'Industrie - Paris  

body: >-

The first event dedicated to innovative savoir-faire

PROTOTYPE aims to be a project accelerator, promoting strategic encounters between creators, companies, and investors. Materials, uses, forms, techniques: the prototypes on display embody the craft of tomorrow, where expertise and innovation come together to create impactful and sustainable solutions.  

A successful first edition!
Over the course of several days, PROTOTYPE brought together creators, researchers, designers, artisans, partners, and visitors around a shared belief: prototypes are essential tools for imagining and building the models of tomorrow. Find the best of the 2026 edition on this website, and see you soon for the next chapter in this collective adventure!

PROTOTYPE - The first trade show dedicated to innovative expertise


"PROTOTYPE is the very embodiment of the projects carried out within the Campus des Métiers et des Qualifications d'Excellence - Design, Materials and Innovation (Campus for Trades and Excellence in Qualifications)—and which we wish to develop within the framework of the project De-MAIN (Future Investment Program) by promoting collaborations between training, research and economic actors. Visiting the fair has already opened up real collaborations."
Nathalie Gillet
Clermont Auvergne INP

"It was a great experience, which taught us a lot and brought us, and already several projects are underway following the show."
Jessica Boubetra
Lumierrante

"I am convinced that this type of initiative, at both the national and regional levels, can highlight the remarkable expertise of our artisan-designers while inspiring new talent. Furthermore, collaborations with the academic research community could be established on topics where the scientific expertise of our laboratories could provide innovative solutions. These sectors of artisanal excellence, which are the very essence of our regions, will thus be able to enhance their visibility, appeal, and performance."
Pierre Gohar
Director of Industrial Relations, Le réseau des Carnot

"I wanted to congratulate you sincerely on PROTOTYPE. The format, the quality of exhibitors and stakeholders, and this dialogue between know-how and innovation have really stimulated me and done me a lot of good. Honestly, this event brought me a real breath: it brought together exactly the universes that are passionate about me and gave me a precious energy. "
Nicolas David
artist-ceramist & designer
